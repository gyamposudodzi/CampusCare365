BITE-SIZE DELIGHTS

— SPECIAL RECIPES MADE FOR YOU —

Jollof Rice
60 mins

Jollof is a popular rice dish that originated in West Africa. It is typically made with rice, tomatoes, onions, and a blend of spices, and can also include meat or vegetables.

Noodles
25 mins

Noodles are a type of pasta that are typically made from wheat flour, water, and salt. They come in a variety of shapes and sizes and can be served in soups, stir-fries, or as a standalone dish.

Fried Rice
35 mins

Fried rice is a dish that originated in China but is now enjoyed around the world. It is typically made with cooked rice, vegetables, eggs, and a variety of seasonings, such as soy sauce and sesame oil.

Omelette
20 mins

Omelette is a dish made from beaten eggs that are cooked in a skillet and folded over a filling, such as cheese, vegetables, or meat. It is a popular breakfast dish around the world and is quick and easy to prepare

Quesadillas
20mins

Quesadillas are a popular Mexican dish made from tortillas that are filled with cheese and other ingredients, such as meat, beans, and vegetables. The tortillas are folded in half and cooked on a griddle or in a skillet until the cheese is melted and the tortilla is crispy.

Tacos
30 mins

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish made with a tortilla that is filled with various ingredients, such as meat, cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, and salsa. They are typically served with a variety of toppings, such as sour cream, guacamole, and hot sauce.

Rice and Beans
45 mins

Rice and beans is a popular dish that is enjoyed in many cultures around the world. It typically consists of cooked rice and beans that are seasoned with spices, herbs, and other ingredients, such as onions, peppers, and tomatoes.

Avocado Toast
10 mins

Avocado toast is a simple and healthy dish that consists of mashed avocado spread on top of a slice of toasted bread. It is often topped with additional ingredients such as salt, pepper, lemon juice, and chili flakes.

Fried Yam and Sauce
45 mins

Fried yam with sauce is a popular West African dish that consists of sliced yam that is deep-fried until crispy and golden brown. It is often served with a spicy sauce made from tomatoes, onions, peppers, and other seasonings.

Breakfast burrito
20 mins

A breakfast burrito is a popular Tex-Mex dish that consists of a flour tortilla filled with scrambled eggs, cheese, and other ingredients, such as bacon, sausage, potatoes, and vegetables. It is often served with salsa or other toppings.

Chocolate mug cake
3 mins

A chocolate mug cake is a quick and easy dessert that can be made in a microwave. It usually consists of flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, milk, and oil mixed together in a mug and microwaved until cooked through.

Fruit Salad
15 mins

A fruit salad is a healthy and refreshing dish made from a variety of chopped fruits, such as strawberries, blueberries, kiwi, and pineapple. It is often served with a dressing made from honey, lime juice, and mint.
